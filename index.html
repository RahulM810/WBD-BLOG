Emerging Threats in 2025

The cybersecurity landscape continues to evolve rapidly, with attackers developing more sophisticated techniques. Here's what you need to watch out for in 2024:

"The average cost of a data breach has risen to $4.45 million in 2023, a 15% increase over three years."

IBM Security Cost of a Data Breach Report 2023

Cyber Attack Statistics

Key Protective Measures

  • Implement multi-factor authentication (MFA) on all accounts
  • Keep all software updated with the latest security patches
  • Educate employees/yourself about phishing recognition
  • Maintain regular backups of critical data
  • Use a reputable password manager
